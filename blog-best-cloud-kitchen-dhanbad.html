What is a Cloud Kitchen?

A cloud kitchen (also called a dark kitchen or ghost kitchen) is a food delivery-only restaurant. There's no dining area — everything is focused on producing fresh, high-quality food and delivering it fast. This model allows kitchens to pass those savings on to customers through better prices and more focus on food quality.

Why Cloud Kitchens Are Taking Over Dhanbad's Food Scene

  • Faster delivery: No dine-in tables means the kitchen runs 100% for delivery orders — faster prep, faster delivery
  • Better hygiene: Cloud kitchens have stricter cleanliness standards since food safety is their entire brand
  • More variety: Without space constraints of a restaurant, cloud kitchens can offer wider menus
  • Lower prices: No rent for a fancy location means savings passed on to you
